Overview

Our newspaper lays a foundation for a greener fairer & wiser future for all. Barring an objective of transforming ideas and initiatives by organizations on the African continent to ensure sustainability through addressing climate change and its impacts to the global community.


This is full of articles written experts artists young people and participants in our activities as we promote environmental sustainability within the global south through arts culture. Publication science and educational research etc.

With more than 100 ideas this edition of or newspaper will benefit more than 50 schools 100 individuals and more than 50 communities all running articles to help them raise funding for their activities and activism.

Safar more than 15 organization in Uganda and beyond have already shared contacts and articles to feature in this edition

Furthermore, this newspaper will be celebrating key important figures in the climate and ecological circle to recognize the efforts for a greener fairer and wiser future.

Also accompanied by an online edition our newspaper will also feature transformation stories and ideas by young people in African.
